Shree Shree Parva

The journey of 'Shree-Shree Parva', the school's annual function has been a beautiful, enriching and joyful one that has the elements of education as well as entertainment. The title of the function is an apt one as it celebrates the glory of two great epitomes of Indian tradition and culture. Lord Shree Krishna and Dr. Shrikant Jichkar, the founder of Our School School. Shree-Shree Parva is the unique cultural heritage of Our School school

Morning Assembly

One of the most attractive and enjoyable features of the school is its well-planned Morning Assembly which comprises of day wise activities such as action songs, shlokas, Geeta Chanting, exercises, yoga, group and table recitations, show n tell activity, puppet shows, skits, talks on various occasions, festival celebration etc.

Read 'N' Grow Inter School Contest

The Read 'N' Grow Contest, a venture to promote the reading habit is the brainchild of the school. It started as an Intra School Contest and has assumed mammoth proportions as an Inter School Contest with students from schools all over Nagpur participating in a written contest held every year. A slew of Competitions like the Poster Making, Certificate Designing, Blurb and Review Writing are held throughout the year culminating in the Interschool event.

Fledgeling Fiesta

The Pre-Primary Annual Day (Fledgeling Fiesta) is the most awaited event. This is the platform where each child gets a chance to display his/her talent. This programme keeps the audience spellbound, mesmerized and glued to their seats.

Annual Projects

The school aims at inculcating social responsibility in its students by involving them in various activities that revolve around a social cause. Down the years the school has undertaken Projects such as Cleanliness, Road Safety Awareness and Care for the Elderly to name a few.

Our Grows Together

It is rightly said that a family that prays together stays together. So, we read, eat and paint together with the aim to socialise, learn, share and enjoy the essence and feeling of oneness.

Visual Arts

Through Visual Arts, we let our students explore and perform their artistic skills, imagination and creativity. We provide opportunities to all students, right from Pre-Primary to Secondary to perceive, respond to, create and communicate through Art and Craft.

Performing Arts

At Our School, we help students bring out their creativity as well as develop their personality. Performing arts at Our School is all about self-expression, exploring other choices and embracing individuality which allows them to develop self-confidence and belief in themselves.

Visits, Tours And Excursions

For promoting learning by observation and involvement, the various educational visits, tours and excursions are organized to places of historical, scientific and social interests in and around Nagpur. Such visits leave an indelible mark on the young minds and mould them towards their mission life.

Sports

What we learn on the ground, cannot be learned in classrooms’’. At Our School, a strong emphasis is laid on sports and physical development. Students have access to various sports activities wherein they can play Basketball, Cricket, Handball, Volleyball, Athletics etc. However, there is a provision of indoor games such as Boxing, Gymnastics, Badminton, Table Tennis, Carrom, Chess etc. Green gym is an added attraction

School Clubs

Students are encouraged to participate in activities organized by various clubs, such as Literary Club, GK Club, Our School Green Club, Arts Club, Health and Wellness Club and Heritage Club. These activities promote and enhance the inherent potential of the students.
